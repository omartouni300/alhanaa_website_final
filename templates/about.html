About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Al Hanaa for Agricultural Development is a pioneering force in Egypt’s agricultural sector. Strategically located at Kilometer 132 on the Asyut Western Desert Road, our operations benefit from fertile lands and ideal growing conditions that enable year-round cultivation and sustainable growth.

We are proud to be part of the El Morshedy Group, a trusted name with over four decades of experience across multiple industries including commodity trading and flour milling.

What We Do

We specialize in cultivating premium fruits and seasonal crops while expanding into value-added products like pickled olives and raisin grapes. Our integrated model ensures full traceability and quality.

Our Vision

To become a leading provider of top-tier agricultural products for domestic and global markets, responsibly grown and tailored to client needs.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, sustainably cultivated products while exceeding customer expectations through innovation and integrity.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: Honesty, fairness, and responsibility in all operations.
  • Sustainability: Commitment to protecting natural resources.
  • Creativity: Encouraging innovation in agriculture.
  • Technology: Using modern tools to boost productivity.
  • Teamwork: Collaboration and shared goals for success.

Why Choose Us?

With over a decade of hands-on experience, international reach, and advanced systems like SAP, we ensure global compliance, consistency, and quality in all we deliver.

Our Global Outlook

Rooted in Egypt and reaching the world, we continue to grow through innovation and dedication to sustainability. With Al Hanaa, you're part of a vision shaping the future of food.
